Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2025-01-29

Introduction

At Video Journal Daily, your privacy is our priority. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect your information.

Data We Collect

Email Address: Used for account management and support.

Encrypted Video Journals & Transcripts: These are encrypted on your device before upload. We never access or process them.

Optional Data (User Selected): Weather, Location Coordinates, Walking Steps, Photos.

None of this data is shared with third parties.

Data Storage & Security

Local Storage: Free users' journals are stored only on their device.

Cloud Storage: Premium users' journals are encrypted and stored on the cloud.

End-to-End Encryption: Your videos and transcripts are encrypted before leaving your device.

No Access to User Data: We do not have access to your video content, even in the cloud.

Deletion Upon Account Deletion: If you delete your account, all associated data is permanently removed.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share any of your personal data with third parties.

Your Rights (GDPR Compliance)

Access your data.

Request deletion of your data.

Correct any incorrect information.

Withdraw consent at any time.

To exercise these rights, email us at videojournaldaily@gmail.com.

Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for users under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. If changes are made, we will notify users through the App.

Contact Information

For questions, contact us at videojournaldaily@gmail.com.
